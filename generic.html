About Me

From then on, my passion with the industry only grew. At the end of 2022 I started my studies at hanzehogeschool groningen in creative media and game technologies. In my free time I enjoy working on my own projects and learning new things. I am familiar and able to work with the SCRUM method and have learned to work with GameMaker Studio, Unity and Unreal engine. I can create 3D models in blender, as well as animations and procedural materials
