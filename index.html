Stick Rampage

Stick rampage is a personal project. I started development in late 2022 and released it in the fall of 2023. The game engine used for the game is Game Maker Studio 2. The game features 48 unique different levels and 12 different enemies for the player to fight against and is as of yet my largest project. It makes use of google play services to automatically coud save player progress. The game is available on the play store for all android devices.

Build the nuke is a personal project i started in the end of 2023.
